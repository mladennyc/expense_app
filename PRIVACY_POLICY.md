## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

- **Password Encryption:** All passwords are hashed using bcrypt (one-way encryption)
- **HTTPS/SSL:** All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
- **Database Security:** Data is stored in encrypted databases
- **Access Controls:** Only authenticated users can access their own data
- **Regular Security Audits:** We regularly review and update our security practices

### 4.2 Data Location

Your data is stored on secure servers. The exact location may vary depending on your hosting provider, but we ensure all data is stored in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

### 4.3 Data Retention

- We retain your data for as long as your account is active

- We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes
